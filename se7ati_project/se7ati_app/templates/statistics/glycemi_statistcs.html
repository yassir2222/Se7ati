Glycemia

What is Glycemia?

Glycemia refers to the concentration of glucose (a type of sugar) in your bloodstream. It's more commonly known as your blood sugar level or blood glucose level.
Fasting Blood Glucose:
mg/dL: 80 to 130 mg/dL
mmol/L: 4.4 to 7.2 mmol/L
Post-Prandial Blood Glucose: (1-2 hours after starting a meal)
mg/dL: Less than 180 mg/dL
mmol/L: Less than 10.0 mmol/L
